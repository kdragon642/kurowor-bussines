Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kuroword (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares personal data when you visit our website, purchase products, or use our services.

1. Data controller

The controller is Kuroword, operating from Spain (European Union). Contact and business details are on our Contact page. Any tax or postal information we must provide under law will match our records with payment providers (Paddle, Stripe) and on invoices.

2. Data we collect

  • Contact and account: name, email, and messages you send us.
  • Transactions: order details, product purchased, amount, and payment status. Payment card data is collected and processed by our payment partners (e.g. Paddle, Stripe); we typically receive a tokenized reference or last digits, not your full card number.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and cookies or similar technologies as described below.
  • Usage: how you use our SaaS or downloads, when needed to provide the service or security.

3. Purposes and legal bases (GDPR / UK)

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on: contract (processing purchases and accounts); legitimate interests (security, analytics, improving services); legal obligation (tax, accounting); and consent where required (e.g. non-essential cookies).

4. Payment processors

We use Paddle and/or Stripe to process payments. They act as independent controllers or processors according to their role and your transaction. Their privacy policies apply to payment data: Paddle Privacy, Stripe Privacy.

5. Cookies

We may use essential cookies for the site to function and, where you consent, analytics or marketing cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings and, where we provide a banner, through your choices there.

6. Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above: e.g. order records for tax and legal compliance (often several years), support emails for a reasonable period, and marketing data until you withdraw consent or object.

7. International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing. You may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise rights, email support@kuroword.com.

9. California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents may have additional rights regarding personal information, including disclosure, correction, deletion, and opt-out of certain sharing. Contact us as above. We do not sell personal information as “sale” is defined under the CCPA for monetary consideration.

10. Children

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect their personal data.

11. Changes

We may update this policy by posting changes here and revising the “Last updated” date.
