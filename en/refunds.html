Refund & cancellation policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This policy describes how Kuroword handles refunds, cancellations, and returns for digital products, SaaS subscriptions, and print-on-demand (POD) goods. Consumer rights under your local law remain unaffected where they cannot be waived.

1. How to request help

Email support@kuroword.com with your order reference, email used at checkout, and a short description. If your payment was processed by Paddle or Stripe, you may also use the support or receipt links they provide.

2. Digital goods (non-subscription)

Because digital content can be duplicated instantly, all sales are generally final once delivery or download access has been granted. We may issue a refund or replacement if:

  • the product is defective or not as described and we cannot fix access within a reasonable time; or
  • duplicate charges or clear payment errors occurred.

Refund requests should be made within 14 days of purchase unless mandatory law requires a longer period.

3. SaaS subscriptions

You may cancel renewal at any time through the method indicated in your account or confirmation email; cancellation stops future charges but does not automatically refund the current period unless required by law or offered at our discretion.

We may refund the last subscription charge if you cancel within 14 days of the initial purchase and have not materially used the service beyond reasonable trial — assessed case by case. Mandatory cooling-off rights in your country apply where applicable.

4. Print on demand (physical products)

POD items are made to order. Change of mind returns are not accepted unless we explicitly offer them at checkout.

We will replace or refund if the item is damaged, defective, or incorrectly printed due to our error. Contact us within 14 days of delivery with photos and your order ID. Return shipping instructions will be provided when a return is required.

5. Chargebacks

Please contact us before disputing a charge with your bank so we can resolve the issue. Unfounded chargebacks may lead to account closure.

6. Processing time

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method. Timing depends on Paddle, Stripe, or your bank (often 5–10 business days).
